The boundary between art and science is often blurred. Both share a common foundation: the desire to understand and represent the world around us. Through various projects, I explore how scientific imagery and concepts can become sources of artistic inspiration.

> "The most beautiful thing we can experience is the mysterious. It is the source of all true art and science."
> — Albert Einstein

Science and art both attempt to capture truth—one through measurement and analysis, the other through emotion and perception. My work sits at this intersection, using artistic techniques to communicate scientific wonder.
